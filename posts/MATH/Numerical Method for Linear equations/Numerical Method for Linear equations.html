Numerical Method for Linear equations

重要

Consider the time-dependent Cauchy probelm of scalar equation,

{ut+aux=0,−∞<x<∞,t≥0u(x,0)=u0(x)

Define:

  • h=Δx
  • k=Δt
  • xj=jh,j=⋯,−1,0,1,⋯
  • tn=nk,n=0,1,2,⋯
  • xj+12=xj+h/2=(j+12)h
  • λ=kh, this is cfl in algorithm

Up-Wind schemes:

ujn+1=ujn−λ2a(uj+1n−uj−1n)+λ2|a|(uj+1n−2ujn+uj−1n)

Lax-Friedrichs

ujn+1=ujn−λ2a(uj+1n−uj−1n)+12(uj+1n−2ujn+uj−1n)

Lax-Wendroff

The general form:

ujn+1=λ+λ22uj−1n+(1−λ2)ujn+λ2−λ2uj+1n

After the expansion:

ujn+1=ujn−λ2a(uj+1n−uj−1n)+12(uj+1n−2ujn+uj−1n)

Beam-Warming

The general form:

ujn+1=fracλ2−λ2uj−1n+λ(2−λ)ujn+(1−λ)(2−λ)2uj+1n
